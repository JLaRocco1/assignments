Chicken

The chicken is pan seared with the most intriguing spcies and seasonings like cumin, roasted cayenne and repper flakes, oregano, freshly ground garlic, a dash of sage, and of course to top it off...ground black pepper and sea salt. The roasted garlic red bliss mashed and sauteed vegetable medly are delicous compliments with this dish. Ask your server about our local seasonal wines that pair excellently as well.
